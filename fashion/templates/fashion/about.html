Meet the Founder

"Fashion is more than just clothing; it is an expression of our deepest identity and heritage."

Founded in 2014, Domemily was born from a passion to bridge the gap between traditional African aesthetics and modern high fashion. Our founder envisioned a brand that empowers individuals to wear their culture with pride and elegance.

Our Journey

A Legacy in the Making

2014

The Beginning

Domemily was founded in Accra with a single sewing machine and a dream to redefine local fashion standards.

2019

Expanding Horizons

We opened our first dedicated studio and began accepting international orders, sharing Ghanaian style with the world.

Today

A Luxury Staple

Recognized as a premier destination for bespoke African luxury, dressing clients for weddings, galas, and special moments.
